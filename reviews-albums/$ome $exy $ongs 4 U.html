Introduction

This Valentine’s Day, we have been met with a highly anticipated collab project from two of Canada’s finest. Rumors of a PND x Drake album have been floating around for a while now but officially got a release date and name on February 3rd, 2025, with quite a bit of hype being garnered from their respective fanbases. These two are no strangers to collaborating with one another having had amazing musical chemistry on tracks in the past such as “Recognize”, “Come and See Me”, and “Since Way Back” which were all very positively received overall. With this latest edition of their joint efforts, we get a full length twenty one track project blending styles of both R&B and Hip-hop.
