My Top 50 Hip-Hop Albums of ALL Time

My personal top 50 picks of the best 50 albums in hip-hop

11/4/2025

So, the other day I was on the phone with a real good friend of mine and the topic of Pitchfork’s “Top 100 Rap Albums” list came up in conversation. For those who know, I crashed out the first time I saw that list because Pitchfork is just SO good at ragebaiting rap music fans that it’s honestly award worthy. In our talk, we actually went through the list and I most definitely kept composure the second time around (I had a mini-crashout). From there we went over and looked at another list from Rolling Stone titled, “The 200 Greatest Hip-Hop Albums of All Time”, which definitely featured a better Top 100’s section if we ignore their 200’s section entirely. The list had some really big hollow points and some… odd selections to say the least which warranted me to tweak out even more. Upon witnessing me damn near foaming at the mouth from these choices and ranking statuses, my friend, Rafael, presented the idea of creating my own list generated from my own thoughts and general preferences combined. In return, he will grant me a list of 100 manga series to read and we will share our consensus in the end. And here we are. Without further ado, I present to you my Top 50 Hip-Hop Albums of ALL Time.
